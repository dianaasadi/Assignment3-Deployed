Sport

My favourite sport is padel.

Padel is a fast-growing racket sport that combines elements of tennis and squash. Played in doubles on an enclosed court slightly smaller than a tennis court, it uses solid rackets and a depressurized ball. The walls are part of the game, adding a unique strategic twist. Padel is easy to learn, social, and popular for all ages, especially in Spain, Latin America, and increasingly across Europe and the Middle East.

Food

Sushi, Mediterranean cuisine

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that has become popular worldwide for its fresh flavors, artistic presentation, and healthy ingredients. It typically consists of vinegared rice combined with raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits. Sushi comes in various forms, such as rolls (maki), hand-pressed pieces (nigiri), or wrapped in seaweed (temaki). Loved for its delicate taste and visual appeal, sushi is more than just food—it’s a cultural experience that highlights balance, precision, and freshness. Mediterranean cuisine, on the other hand, is rooted in the coastal regions of countries like Greece, Italy, Spain, and parts of the Middle East and North Africa. It emphasizes the use of fresh vegetables, fruits, legumes, whole grains, olive oil, fish, and lean meats. Known for both its flavor and health benefits, this cuisine often features dishes like hummus, grilled seafood, salads with feta cheese, olives, and herb-rich stews. Mediterranean food reflects a lifestyle centered around community, simplicity, and nourishment, making it one of the healthiest and most enjoyable diets in the world.
